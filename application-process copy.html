Application Process

​​​How to Apply in the Taiwan

Step - 1

Free consultation and application advice

Free 1-on-1 Consultation

  • Free study abroad application consultation
  • Upper School Service Introduction
  • Consultant experience sharing

Provide initial free study abroad consultation, learn about the applicant's background, grades and study abroad plan during the consultation, give advice on studying abroad, and share the consultant's past experience in assisting students in application.

* The free consultation period is limited, excluding school selection analysis and strategic planning

Step - 2

Payment and confirmation of entrustment

Complete Payment

  • Selected agency plan
  • Confirm entrusted payment
  • Complete the Study Abroad Application Agreement

Select a plan that meets your needs, and contact the consultant to confirm the commission. Upper School accepts cash, bank transfer and credit card to pay the agency fee. After the fee is confirmed, the consultant will contact the applicant to complete the signing of the study abroad application agreement.

Step - 3

Application goals and strategy formulation

Maximizing Your Chances

  • Application background condition assessment
  • Exclusive application schedule
  • Communication group instant service

The selection consultant will confirm with the applicant the study plan of the department and other departments, set the relevant schedule according to the goal to be achieved, and keep abreast of your study abroad application plan; at the same time, the upper school team communication group will be established to keep in touch with you and update the progress at any time.

Step - 4

School selection consultation and analysis

Ideal Schools Selection

  • Preliminary school selection consultation
  • Objective school selection evaluation
  • Finalist application school

Understand the applicant's background, conditions and needs, and combine the school ranking, school reputation and environment to analyze the applicant and select suitable schools. After primary selection, re-election and final selection, the final application list will be discussed with the applicants.

Step - 5

Writing and editing documents for studying abroad

Editing and Proofreading

  • Exquisite document packaging
  • Foreign Editing Professional Xiurun
  • journalist interview

Strictly select foreign editors to create exclusive Essay, SOP, CV and recommendation letters with native language thinking. Upper School consultants have many years of experience and are good at guiding students to find application highlights, plus in-depth reporter-style interviews with editors to maximize the advantages of applicants.

* Document writing and editing will vary depending on the plan

Step - 6

Formal application and document dispatch

Completing Your Application

  • Study Abroad Application Form Assistance
  • Additional grades, sending documents on behalf of
  • Application follow-up progress tracking

Additional third-party score certification (WES, ECE) assistance such as TOEFL / IELTS, GRE / GMAT, SAT/ACT, etc., and international express delivery services are provided according to the school's requirements. Application form process assistance, post-application counseling and school contact and progress tracking.

Step - 7

Admission follow-up services

Post Admit Assistance

  • Final selection school
  • Documents required for visa application
  • Relevant timetable after confirmation of admission

Based on factors such as student interests, course suitability and employment opportunities, the final school will be selected. According to the requirements of the admission school, assist students to complete the tasks required before departure and obtain the documents required for visa application.

Step - 8

Pre-departure preparation service

Pre-departure Assistance

  • Student Dormitory Application
  • co-sponsor student visa
  • Pre-departure explanation service

Assist with all pre-departure services before entry: school dormitory application, student visa counseling/application, air ticket pick-up arrangements, free agency in the language center, pre-departure explanation services, etc., so that you can prepare for departure with peace of mind.
